5 Summits of the Lost Land

↡

The Conquering Warrior, whose name was lost in the annals of time, had seen the world crumble before his eyes. The once mighty empires had fallen, the oceans had risen to swallow the lands, and the skies had turned black with ash and smoke. In this world of destruction, the only hope for survival lay in the promise land, a place where life still thrived and where the warrior hoped to rebuild civilization.

↡

... But the road to the promise land was long and treacherous, for it lay beyond five mountains of destruction, each one more challenging than the last. The Conquering Warrior knew that he had to conquer these mountains if he wanted to reach his destination.
